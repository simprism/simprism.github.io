About me



My story

I am a China-based technology reporter focused on finding and telling stories using data. I am currently based in Shanghai, and write for TechNode, a leading English language tech media outlet where I cover artificial intelligence, mobility, and cybersecurity. I am confident using Python and its various analysis and visualization libraries to gain insights in data that may otherwise go unnoticed.


I completed my studies at Stellenbosch University, South Africa, in 2013, during which time I was both a layout artist and the art director of departmental publications. I also co-founded an online magazine, to which numerous photographers and journalists, from South Africa and abroad, contributed.


Shortly after completing my studies I started reporting for a pan-African technology and startup news service, providing me with valuable insight into African economics and politics through the lens of technology. Here, I reported extensively on the rise of bitcoin, providing analysis of the cryptocurrency's uses and adoption on the continent.
